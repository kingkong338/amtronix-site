🔧 ECM Repair & Replacement

The Engine Control Module (ECM) is the brain of your vehicle. It controls essential functions like fuel injection, ignition timing, and more. If it fails, your engine performance suffers or stops entirely.

🛠️ Our ECM Services Include:
  • Diagnosis: Accurate fault detection using advanced scanners.
  • Repair: We fix burnt circuits, water damage, and software errors.
  • Replacement: Genuine ECMs programmed for your vehicle’s specs.
🚗 Symptoms of a Faulty ECM:
  • Car won’t start or randomly stalls
  • Check Engine Light always on
  • Poor mileage or misfiring
  • Transmission or shifting issues

🛞 EPS (Electric Power Steering) Repair

The Electric Power Steering (EPS) system helps you steer with minimal effort. When it malfunctions, you may notice heavy steering, jerky movements, or complete assist loss.

🚨 Common Symptoms:
